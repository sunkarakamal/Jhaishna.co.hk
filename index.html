Database Management

We blend our technology and business expertise, leveraging a proven and repeatable methodology to deliver expert Oracle DW/BI Project Consulting. Our related professional services helping organizations gain greater business value from all their data – on premise, with partners, or in the cloud.

Data Management and Data Governance initiatives won’t be sustainable if they aren’t well aligned with business goals to serve a higher purpose. Jhaishna creates data management solutions by digging deep into your business until we gain specific insight to your perspective, what you really want to achieve and what you’re up against. Our dedicated staff of full-time Data Management consultants combines this insight with extensive expertise to ensure your projects meet your needs and are successfully delivered.

Experienced in database tuning and performance monitoring to ensure database performs optimally at all times. And got expertise in database capacity planning taking into database growth and changes for implementing archiving and database growth stabilisation procedures.

We want to partner with your team to build and deliver on both your short and long-term strategic data management project needs.

IT CONSULTANCY/ADVISORY SERVICES

Jhaishna IT Advisor is here to help businesses leverage first class, enterprise-level IT support without the significant investment of maintaining in-house IT resources. This one-stop professional IT management service has everything an organisation needs to free up resources for core business growth, hassle-free.

Jhaishna’s IT specialists are capable of handling all aspects of onsite support, including:
• General PC onsite/remote support including basic hardware/software troubleshooting and repair
• Data backup and recovery
• Managing network, server, storage and mobile desktop virtualisation
• Hardware & software setup, configuration & upgrades
• Network monitoring & performance management
• Comprehensive network security & infrastructure services

Engaging Models

Customer-centric Engagement Model: We build our expert teams around your domain and technology requirements, offering specialized services and solutions that meet the distinct needs of your business success.

Global Delivery Model:
Our unique global engagement model allows you to choose the sourcing strategy best suited to your business needs. We take a follow-the-sun approach, meaning that no matter where your business is located, we help you keep it running 24/7, while providing a seamless experience across all operations.

Onsite Model:
All the design/development activities are performed by our consultants at the client's site.

Offsite Model:
All activities are performed by one of Jhaishna’s staff in an offsite location.

Offshore Model:
All activities are performed by Jhaishna’s staff in an offsite, which is located in a different country. Jhaishna’s team will work with the client to understand the business requirements, define the scope, and control quality throughout the project lifecycle
